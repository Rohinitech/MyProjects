Event hall decoration

A wedding hall decoration using flowers typically entails utilizing floral arrangements to create a stunning and romantic ambiance for the celebration.
The floral theme, types of flowers chosen, and their placement throughout the venue.
It may include details such as centerpiece designs for tables, floral arches or backdrops for the ceremony area, flower garlands for the aisle, and other decorative elements like hanging floral installations or flower walls.
This would also specify the color palette and overall style of the floral arrangements to ensure they complement the wedding theme and enhance the beauty of the space.
