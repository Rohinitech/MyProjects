Essential oil and it's benefits

*All Essential oil are approved by FDA*

1. Rose Essential Oil:
Rose oil has been utilized for cosmetic and skin care applications. This essential rose's deep and uplifting floral perfume smells just like a fresh rose flower.

2. Lavender Essential Oil:
Lavender oil is also useful for promoting sleep, releasing stress, easing depression, relaxing nerves, and alleviating the symptoms of headaches and migraines, making it a vital component of any aromatherapy kit.

3. Tea tree oil:
Tea tree oil has been used as a traditional medicine by the Aboriginal people of Australia for centuries. These native Australians crush tea tree leaves to extract the oil, then inhale it to treat coughs and colds or apply it directly to the skin for healing.

4. Eucalyptus oil:
It can also relieve pain and fight against herpes simplex virus thanks to its antimicrobial and anti-inflammatory properties.

5. Lemon oil:
Lemon oil is safe for aromatherapy and topical use. This essential oil can kill harmful bacteria that can grow on your skin.

6. Rosemary oil:
Rosemary oil is safe to use in aromatherapy and topically on the skin with a carrier oil. If you’re pregnant or have epilepsy or high blood pressure, it’s advised to avoid using rosemary oil.

7. Cedarwood oil:
Cedarwood oil, which has antioxidant and antibacterial properties, is a popular ingredient in insect repellent, shampoo and deodorant with its woodsy scent. You can also use cedarwood oil to help with sleep and anxiety.
